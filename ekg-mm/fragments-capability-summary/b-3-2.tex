% B.3.2 Business Rules -- Summary
%
Data Quality business rules\index{business!rules}\index{data!quality!rules} ensure that the data is
fit for its intended purpose.\index{fit for purpose}
\glslocalreset{sme}\Glspl{sme} specify the criteria used to validate and enforce data integrity.\index{data!integrity}
The criteria are translated into agreed specifications (i.e. business rules) which are later codified for
\iindex{data!profiling} or measuring conformity.
\improve{JG>Business Rules should be related to Use Cases}
Data quality business rules can also be embedded into data capture systems to ensure validity at source.
