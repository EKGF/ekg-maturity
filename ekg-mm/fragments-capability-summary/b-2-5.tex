% B.2.5 Data Integration & Interoperability -- Summary
%
Data integration\index{data!integration} is the process of combining data from different sources into a single,
\iindex{unified view} for business consumption and enhanced utility.
The process of integrating data from multiple sources begins with the ingestion process and may include activities
such as data profiling, cleansing/remediation, cross-referencing transformation, and field mapping.
