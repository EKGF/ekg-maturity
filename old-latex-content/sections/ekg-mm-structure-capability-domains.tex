\subsection{Business Pillar}\label{subsec:ekg-maturity-the-four-pillars-business-pillar}

All Business-side capabilities including~\nameref{ch:ekg-maturity-business-strategy-actuation},
\index{business!strategy}~\nameref{ch:ekg-maturity-business-model-elaboration},
\index{business!model!elaboration}~\nameref{ch:ekg-maturity-business-enablers}, Alignment,
\nameref{sec:ekg-maturity-business-operating-model} and others.
Does not include all the actual Business Capabilities\index{business!capabilities} that
an enterprise may have themselves.

Addresses the \iindex{audience} of personas on the business-side of an enterprise, C-level,
\gls{lob} execs,\index{executive}\index{persona!executive}
corporate planners,\index{corporate planner}\index{persona!corporate planner}
business architects\index{business!architects}, product owners\index{product owner}\index{persona!product owner},
management consultants\index{management consultant}\index{persona!management consultant} and so forth.

\subsection{Organization Pillar}\label{subsec:ekg-maturity-the-four-pillars-organization-pillar}

All relevant Organizational Capabilities including~\nameref{ch:ekg-maturity-executive-leadership},
~\nameref{ch:ekg-maturity-product-ownership},
~\nameref{ch:ekg-maturity-delivery-management},
~\nameref{ch:ekg-maturity-organizational-culture}, and
~\nameref{ch:ekg-maturity-organizational-capabilities}.

Addresses the \iindex{audience} of people that are neither business, data nor tech such as financial execs and experts, risk
execs and experts, program/portfolio/project managers, HR execs and experts and so forth.

\subsection{Data Pillar}\label{subsec:ekg-maturity-the-four-pillars-data-pillar}

All Data (Management) capabilities including~\nameref{ch:ekg-maturity-data-strategy}.
\index{strategy!data strategy}

Addresses the \iindex{audience} of people in the data-management and data-governance departments.

\subsection{Technology Pillar}\label{subsec:ekg-maturity-the-four-pillars-technology-pillar}

All Technology Capabilities including~\nameref{ch:ekg-maturity-technology-strategy},
~\nameref{ch:ekg-maturity-technology-execution}, and
~\nameref{ch:ekg-maturity-user-interface}.
\index{strategy!technology strategy}

Addresses the \iindex{audience} of technologists, technical architects, developers, infrastructure execs and experts,
security execs and experts etc.
