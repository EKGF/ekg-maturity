# About

The EKG Maturity Model is developed and maintained by the
[Enterprise Knowledge Graph Foundation](https://ekgf.org) (EKGF).

## Related Initiatives

- [EKG Principles](https://principles.ekgf.org) --- the vision and principles for EKG
- [EKG Method](https://method.ekgf.org) --- the Use Case Tree Method
- [EKG Catalog](https://catalog.ekgf.org) --- use cases and their ontologies

## Get Involved

The EKG Maturity Model is open source and welcomes contributions.
Visit the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/EKGF/ekg-maturity) to get involved.
